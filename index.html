About the Congress

The International Student Congress at the International Balkan University brings together students, researchers, and academics from around the globe to share ideas, present research, and collaborate on innovative solutions to global challenges.

This year's congress theme focuses on "The Future is now: AI, Sustainability, and Ethics in a Rapidly Evolving World" exploring how students can contribute to creating a more sustainable and equitable future through research and innovation.

Join us for engaging discussions, keynote speeches, workshop sessions, and networking opportunities that will enhance your academic journey and connect you with peers from diverse backgrounds.

Submission Guidelines

Abstract Submission Guidelines

Format Specifications

  • Length: Maximum 350 words
  • File Format: Microsoft Word Document
  • Language: English only
  • Font: Times New Roman, 12-point font (title in 14-point font)
  • Line Spacing: 1.5 line spacing
  • Page Format: A4 page size with margins of 1 inch/2.54 cm on all sides
  • Content: Must contain a clear outline of the theoretical framework, theme, or work in discussion

Submission Methods

  • Email: Submit to ibuisc@ibu.edu.mk
  • Website: Submit via Abstract Form

Full Paper Submission Guidelines

Technical Specifications

  • Length: 3,000-6,000 words
  • Format: A4 page size, 1-inch/2.54 cm margins on all sides
  • Typography: Times New Roman 12pt, double-spaced
  • File Type: Microsoft Word Document
  • Content: Must contain a clear outline of the theoretical framework, theme, or work in discussion

Submission Methods

  • Email: Submit to ibuisc@ibu.edu.mk
  • Website: Submit via Full Paper Form

Full papers should be submitted upon acceptance. The abstracts and papers will be reviewed by International Scientific Committee.

Publication & Proceedings

Congress Proceedings

All accepted papers will be published in the IBU International Student Congress Proceedings 2025 with an official ISBN number. The proceedings will be available in both digital and print formats.

Special Journal Publications

Based on the congress theme, exceptional papers will be published in special editions of the journals of the respective Faculties at International Balkan University:

  • Trends in Economics, Finance and Management (TEFMJ) - Faculty of Economics and Administrative Sciences
  • Journal of Law and Politics - Faculty of Law
  • International Journal of Education and Philology (IJEP) - Faculty of Education & Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences
  • International Journal of Technical and Natural Science (IJTNS) - Faculty of Engineering
  • International Journal of Art and Design (IJAD) - Faculty of Art and Design
